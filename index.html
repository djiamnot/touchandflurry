Touch & Flurry

Program note


orbiting comet
simmers frosty dust

a fingertip
brushes bare skin

spreads
down the path
a morning dew


Comète en orbite
Bouillon de poussière glaciale

Étale
Sur son chemin
La rosée

Performance description

Touch & Flurry is a comprovisation for a pair of robotic instruments by Productions Totem Contemporain called The Pipe . The instruments are played by an innovative controller called Intonaspacio designed by Mailis Rodrigues.
